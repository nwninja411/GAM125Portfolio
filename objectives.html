Objectives

Objective 1

Design and assemble pre-production materials for digital video projects.

Objective 2

Work in a creative leadership role on a minimum of one (1) complete digital project produced in the Production Studio courses. Creative leadership roles include director, writer, producer, editor, cinematographer, sound designer, visual effects supervisor, compositing supervisor, art director, production designer, or graphic designer.

Objective 3

Create visuals that exhibit professional quality in terms of camerawork, composition, and lighting.

Objective 4

Integrate composite shots into complete works by employing industry-standard tools and techniques.

Objective 5

Edit and color grade digital projects using industry-standard nonlinear editors and post-production tools.

Objective 6

Record, edit, and mix high-quality audio, including dialogue, sound effects, and music, which are integrated into a complete digital production.
